Here is the answer of question.

What is AI?

Answer:

AI ka full form hota hai Artificial Intelligence, jiska matlab hai "Banaayi gayi buddhi" ya "machine ki sochne samajhne ki taqat."

🔍 AI kya hota hai?

AI ek technology hai jisme machines, specially computers, ko is tarah se train kiya jaata hai ki wo insaanon ki tarah soch sakein, samajh sakein, seekh sakein aur faisle le sakein.

  • 📱 Keyboard prediction
  • 🎥 YouTube recommendations
  • 🤖 ChatGPT jaise chatbot

🧠 AI ka kaam kaise hota hai?

AI ko bohot saara data diya jaata hai, fir wo patterns seekhta hai aur decisions leta hai.

Example: 10,000 dog aur cat images se AI samajhne lagta hai ki nayi image mein dog hai ya cat.

🧠 Types of AI:

  • Narrow AI: Siri, Alexa
  • General AI: Abhi tak bana nahi hai
  • Super AI: Insaan se bhi intelligent – future possibility

⚙️ AI ka istemal kahaan hota hai?

  • 📱 Mobile phones
  • 📢 Social media
  • 🏥 Healthcare
  • 🏦 Banking
  • 🚗 Self-driving cars

😯 AI humare jeevan mein kya badlav la raha hai?

  • ✅ Kaam asaan
  • ⚡ Speed aur accuracy
  • ⚠️ Jobs ka risk

Simple shabdon mein:
AI ek machine ka dimaag hai, jo data se seekhta hai aur decision leta hai.
